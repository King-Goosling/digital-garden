# Natural Hazards
are naturally occuring events that
- **Threaten** human lives
- Cause **damage** to property.

## Types of natural hazards
### Tectonic
- Earthquakes
- Volcanic eruptions
### Climatic/Climate-related
- Floods
- Storms

## Cyclones vs Typhoon vs Hurricane vs Tornado
### Cyclones (start on sea)
- Typhoon (East)
- Hurricane (West)
