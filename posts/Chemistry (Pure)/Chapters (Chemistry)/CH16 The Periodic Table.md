# The Periodic Table
## Alkali Metals
Down the group:
1. $\downarrow$ m.p. and b.p.
    - $\uparrow$ electron shell 
    - $\uparrow$ atomic radius
    - $\uparrow$ distance between metal atom and another atom
    - $\downarrow$ electrostatic forces of attraction between ==metal atom== and ==another atom==
    - $\downarrow$ amount of energy to overcome them
2. $\uparrow$ density 
    - $\uparrow$ protons 
    - $\uparrow$ mass
3. $\uparrow$ reactivity 
    - $\uparrow$ electron shell 
    - $\downarrow$ electrostatic forces of attraction between ==(protons in) nucleus== and ==electrons==
    - $\uparrow$ ease to lose one electron and obtain a full electronic configuration

### Properties
1. React with $H_2O$ to form a hydroxide and $H_2$ 

## Halogens
Down the group:
1. $\uparrow$ m.p. and b.p.
    - $\uparrow$ electron shell 
    - $\uparrow$ atomic radius
    - $\uparrow$ intermolecular forces of attraction between halogen and ==another atom==
    - $\uparrow$ amount of energy to overcome them
2. $\downarrow$ reactivity 
    - $\uparrow$ electron shell 
    - $\downarrow$ electrostatic forces of attraction between ==(protons in) nucleus== and ==electrons==
    - $\uparrow$ difficulty to gain one electron and obtain a full electronic configuration
3. Appearance darkens
    - E.g.: Chlorine (==greenish-yellow gas==), Bromine (==reddish-brown liquid==), Iodine (==purplish-black solid==), Astatine (==black solid==)

| Halogen           | Colour          | Physical state |
| ----------------- | --------------- | -------------- |
| Chlorine ($Cl_2$) | greenish-yellow | gas            |
| Bromine ($Br_2$)  | reddish-brown   | liquid         |
| Iodine ($I_2$)    | purplish-black  | solid          |
| Astatine ($At_2$) | black           | solid          |
>[!tip] Halogens VS Halides
>Halogens: ==No charge==
>Halides: ==Has a + charge== (anionic forms of halogens)
## Transition Metals
1. $\uparrow$ m.p., b.p. and density
2. Varying oxidation states (EXCEPT $Ag^+$ and $Zn^{2+}$)
3. Form coloured compounds

| Compound                                | Colour     | State    |
| --------------------------------------- | ---------- | -------- |
| Chromium(III) chloride ($CrCl_3$)       | green      | solution |
| Potassium dichromate(VI) ($K_2Cr_2O_7$) | orange     | solution |
| Iron(II) sulfate ($FeSO_4$)             | pale green | solution |
| Iron(III) chloride ($FeCl_3$)           | yellow     | solution |
| Manganese(IV) oxide ($MnO_4$)           | black      | solid    |
| Potassium manganate(VII) ($KMnO_4$)     | purple     | solution |
| Copper(I) oxide ($Cu_2O$)               | red        | solid    |
| Copper(II) carbonate ($CuCO_3$)         | green      | solid    |
| Copper(II) oxide ($CuO$)                | black      | solid    |
